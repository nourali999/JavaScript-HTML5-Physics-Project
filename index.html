A ball is thrown upwards with an initial velocity of 8.00 m/s at an angle of 30 degrees with horizontal from a cliff of height 5.00 m above the ground. The ball lands on the ground. How far from the base of the cliff does the ball land if you are on a planet with half the mass of earth but the same radius.

  • Intial Velocity = 8 m/s
  • Initial Velocity x direction = 8cos(30) = 6.9 m/s
  • Initial Velocity y direction = 8sin(30) = 4 m/s
  • Change in y = -5 m
